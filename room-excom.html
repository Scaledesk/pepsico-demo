Excom Room

How to Extend Laptop Screen and Place a Video Call in XCOM Room ?
Step 1. Put your Laptop on the Docking Station and lock it.
Step 2 : Right click on desktop screen and choose Graphic Option ~~> Output To ~~> Extended Desktop ~~> Digital Display + Digital Television
Step 3 : Now your Laptop screen is Extended to the Monitor and TV. Open your Skype and Place a Video Call. The Camera on the TV will be used for the Video Call.
Note : To change the Camera go to the settings or click on the gear icon of your Skype than go to tools and select Video Device Setting and select the camera that you want to use."

How to place a voice call using Softphone?
A softphone allows you to make audio call using your laptop. This software is installed just below your Skype. To make a call using softphone follow the steps
Step 1. Login into your Skype
Step 2. Enter the Phone Number in the number bar and press the Call Button and your call will be placed.
Note: Kindly use earphones for better user experience."
