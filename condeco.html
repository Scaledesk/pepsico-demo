Condeco Room Booking Functionality

How to book a room using Condeco?
There are two options available for booking a room using the Condeco.
First option is to book from your Desktop.
Follow these steps to book a room using web -
Step 1. Go to URL - http://meeting on your web browser.
Step 2. This will lead you to Welcome Screen. N ow click “ Find a Room” on this screen.
Step 3. Choose building name, floor and fill the date and time and click on the “F ind” button.
Step 4. This will lead you to page where Available Rooms a re shown. You can choose the room you want and book it using the book button.
Step 5. Fill in the Information a bout the meeting and send invites to attendees from attendees tab and press Book & Close to book the room.
Note : You can manage Your Bookings from your bookings page.

The another option to book a room is by Using Condeco Tabs placed outside the meeting rooms.
Step 1. Go to the room that you want to book, Select Time Slot from the Condeco touch panel.
Step 2. Press the confirm button and enter your PIN or swipe your access card and press the confirm button to book the room the selected time slot.

For First Time Card Registration :
Wave the Card in front of the Tab. It will ask for the PIN. Enter the PIN and your Card will be activated to book rooms via Condeco Tab.

Note : The Time slots marked as Green on the condeco screen shows that the room is available.
Red light on the condeco screen shows that the room is currently booked and the meeting is going on.
