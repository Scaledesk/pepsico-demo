Meeting Room

How to make a video call in meeting room using your laptop?
Step 1. Find the appropriate connecting cable (HDMI, Mini HDMI or VGA cable) from the cable cubby and connect it with your laptop.
Step 2. Connect the USB cable to your laptop, this will enable the camera over the TV screen.
Step 3. Change the source mode of the TV to HDMI or other suitable mode using its remote to mirror your screen.
Step 4. Start a Skype call from your laptop, this will enable you to see far endâ€™s view on the screen and the logitech camera will show your view to the far end.
Note : To change the Camera go to the settings or click on the gear icon of your Skype than go to tools and select Video Device Setting and select the camera that you want to use.

How to make an audio conferencing using Trio device?
Step 1. Sign in into the Trio device using your email address.
Step 2. Select place a call option and dial the number using keypad or select person from contacts and you can start your video conferencing.
