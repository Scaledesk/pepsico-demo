Meeting Room

How to make a video call in 4 Pax Meeting room using Visual Plus?
Step 1. Sign in into the Trio device using your email address.
Step 2. Select place a call option and dial the number using keypad or select person from contacts and place a call.
Your video call will start now. You can see the far end’s view on the TV screen.

How to share your content in the meeting room?
Step 1. Find the appropriate connecting cable (HDMI, Mini HDMI or VGA Cable) and connect it with your laptop.
Step 2. Change the source mode of the TV to HDMI or other suitable mode using its remote to share the content.

To connect the Trio device with your phone, follow the steps -
Step 1. Enable the bluetooth of Trio device.
Step 2 – Make a call through your phone and use Trio as the bluetooth speaker & mic.
