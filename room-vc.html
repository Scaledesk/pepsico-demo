VC Room

"How to do Video Teleconferencing in VC Room"
Step 1. Choose VTC mode from the panel. In this mode, both TV screens will be on.
Step 2. Choose Video from Polycom IP 700 Device and enter the IP address (or Skype ID) and Press Green Call button and you can start video conferencing now.

"To start a call using the polycom remote follow these steps -
Step 1 : Choose “Place a Call” option on the Left TV screen using the Polycom remote
Step 2 : Enter the IP address or Skype ID and press the green button to start the call.
Note: You can also use the Remote to adjust the Camera angles.

"To share the content in this mode follow these steps -
Step 1. Find and connect the appropriate cable (Mini HDMI or VGA) from the Cable Cubby.
Step 2. Press the relative button to share the content.

There are two other modes available in this room.
1. PPT - This mode is used for local presentation. You can share your content on the screen as shown previously in the video.
2. MEET - This mode is used for local meeting only. Only few lights will be on in this mode.
NOTE: Use the OFF mode when your meeting is over.
