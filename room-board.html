Board Room

"How to start a video conferencing in the boardroom?"
Step 1. Choose the LINK mode from the touch panel.
Step 2. To share your content of the screen connect your laptop with the appropriate cables (HDMI, Mini HDMI, VGA or Wireless Presenter) available at the center of the table.
Step 3. After connecting cable, press relative button.
Step 4. Connect the USB cable with your laptop.
Step 5. To start the video conferencing, open your Skype and place a call. In this mode your content will be shared on the right TV screen and the far endâ€™s view will be displayed in the left TV screen.
Note: To connect your laptop using Barco wireless device, connect it with your laptop and press its button or use ClickShare App."

How to use the content monitor?
Step 1. To connect your laptop with the content monitor, use the grey USB cable and connect it with your laptop.
Step 2. Use the navigation buttons to adjust the screen as per your requirement.
Step 3. If the touch panel is not required, press the down key and hold it until the screen gets straight and starts going down."

There are two other modes available in this room.
1. PPT - This mode is used for local presentation. You can share your content on the screen as shown previously in the video.
2. MEET - This mode is used for local meeting only. Only few lights will be on in this mode.
Note: Please press OFF mode when your meeting is over.
Note: You can change modes from the touch panel available on the table.
