How to place a voice call using Softphone?
A softphone allows you to make audio call using your laptop. This software is installed just below your Skype.

To make a call using softphone follow the steps
Step 1. Login into your Skype
Step 2. Enter the Phone Number in the number bar and Press the Call Button

And your call will be placed.
Note: Kindly use earphones for better user experience
