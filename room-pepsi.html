Pepsi Room

"How to start video conferencing in the link room?"
Step 1. Select the LINK mode from the panel.
Step 2. Come to the touch panel and login into the Skype.
Step 3. Choose from your contacts to start your video conferencing.

"How to connect your laptop with the screens?"
Step 1. Find the appropriate cable (HDMI, MIni HDMI, VGA or Wireless Presenter) and connect it with your laptop.
Step 2. Choose the relative mode button and your content will be displayed on the screen.
Note: To connect your laptop with the wireless presenter, use the MirrorOP Sender software.

"How to use the content monitor?"
Step 1. To connect your laptop with the content monitor, use the grey USB cable and connect it with your laptop.
Step 2. Use the navigation buttons to adjust the screen as per your requirement.
Step 3. If the touch panel is not required, press the down key and hold it until the screen gets straight and starts going down.
