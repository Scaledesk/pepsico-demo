Training Room

"The training room could be utilized for 3 purposes"
1. Delivering Classroom Training - This mode is used for local training to use this mode choose PPT mode from the panel.
In this mode two projectors will be on. To S hare Your Content on the screen, connect your laptop using HDMI Cable, Mini HDMI Cable, VGA Cable or Wireless Presenter. After connecting your laptop, choose the button from the podium to share the content on the projector screens.
2. Delivering Classroom cum Virtual Training - This mode is used for online learning. To use this mode choose VTC mode from the panel and select Video Mode Learning from the touch panel.
3. Attending Virtual sessions - This mode is used for online teaching purpose. To use this mode choose VTC mode from the panel and select Video Mode Learning from the touch panel.
In this mode two projectors, back screen and camera will be on. The far end attendees can see local view on their screen through this camera, and the local tutor can see far end’s view in the screen.

"How to make a video call in the training room?"
Step 1. Choose VTC mode from the panel.
Step 2. Choose Learning/ Teaching from the touch panel
Step 3. Press call button and enter the IP address of the person and again press call button to start your call.

There’s a whiteboard in this room for the tutor. To show the content of whiteboard on the projector screens, a button is available nearby the board.
To take the printout of whiteboard screen press green button available on the printer."
